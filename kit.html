WHY EMAIL

Why You Need To Go All In On Email Newsletter

Social platforms are rented land. Email is the only channel where you own the relationship.

1

Own Your Audience & Stop Relying on Algorithms

Without email, you're starting from zero every single month. You need to build a bridge between your content and your offer using a strategically designed email system. With a newsletter, you get full control — contact your subscribers whenever and however you want, without competing with algorithms, advertisers, and ever-changing policies.

2

Get 1st-Party Data & Personalise Your Offers

Good luck running polls or asking your viewers for feedback in the comments. With an email newsletter, you can get 1st-party data directly from your subscribers right when they subscribe. Use this data to immediately turn them into buyers and create better content that actually resonates.

3

Generate Revenue Behind The Scenes Without Being Salesy

Selling your products in videos and posts can feel like a chore — and your audience can tell. With email, you generate revenue without ever having to shout out your products in your content. And the conversion rate is over 10x higher compared to other channels.
